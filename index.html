Catherine Jue

Tea brewer for Tekuno • @teawithtekuno

Catherine is a technology and consumer goods entrepreneur bridging human-centered design, first principles problem solving, pointed storytelling, and holistic systems thinking. Driving her work is the belief that the future of our planet lies in localized ecosystems, consistently prioritizing long term results over short term gains, and incorporating nature’s ephemerality into modern day society.

Catherine is the founder of Tekuno, a direct-to-consumer company showcasing sensorially rich Japanese teas directly from independent and multi-generational tea farmers. She is building a lifelong career portfolio that supports:

  • - Sustainable food systems
  • - Design's inherent value
  • - Architecture as permanence
  • - Decommodifying consumer essentials
  • - Microgrids & renewable energy
  • - Art as cultural significance
  • - Benign AGI
